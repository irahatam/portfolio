Gofood

ROLES: Product Researcher and UX Designer

TOOLS: Figma

DURATION: 13 Weeks

Project Description

This project is a part of my Human-Computer Interaction class at New York University. Throughout the semester, I studied the product extensively from doing a usability evaluation to designing a prototype. I am not affiliated with Gojek or Gofood in any way.

About the Product

Gofood is an instant food delivery service within the on-demand, multi-service platform, and digital payment technology company Gojek. Gofood was one of the four services that was a part of the initial Gojek app that launched in 2015.

Product Constraints

After doing initial research on the product, I have noted several constraints:

  • A stable internet connection and a smartphone is required to access the service
  • At the time of research, Gofood is only available in Indonesia (parent product Gojek is available in 7 SE Asian countries)
  • Higher service fees compared to similar product from other companies/competition
  • App size is considered to be relatively big
  • many older users are confused with the modern interface
  • No recommendations based on last/frequent orders
User Research

How might we support users that wants to make group orders?

How might we help users with tracking their monthly expenses and orders efficiently?

How might we make it easier for users that have dietary/allergen needs to order?
