SAP Concur Internship

ROLE: Digital Experience Intern

TOOLS: Figma, SharePoint, Smartsheets

DURATION: 3 Months (June to August 2022)

Project Description

I'm an intern for SAP Concur's digital experince team within digital marketing. I'm in charge of creating a documentation site, updating/designing new enablement materials, creating roadmaps, conducting end-to-end A/B testing project, and driving the digital hub migration project. If you'd like to learn more about my experience don't hesitate to message me!

Project Management

Project Summary

Migrating and redesigning Digital Hub, a site that contains information and enablement materials of teams within the digital marketing scope.

Project Goals
  • Efficiency: creating a seamless process and experience for global field marketers, internal teams, and other stakeholders when visiting the site
  • Sustainability: reducing digital carbon footprint by reducing redundant pages and data stored on the site
  • User Experience: improve stakeholder satisfaction through the utility and ease of use provided in the interaction with the Digital Hub
