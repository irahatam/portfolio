Bunkie

ROLE(S): UX Designer & Researcher [Team of 3]

TOOLS(S): Figma

DURATION: 5 Weeks (Sep 2019 to Oct 2019)

Project Description

54% of the world’s population live in urban communities, the average age for men is 29 (up from 26 two decades ago) and for women is 27 (up from 23 in the same time period). Given these trends, city dwellers tend to spend most of their twenties living with roommates. Find and keeping a good roommate, however, gets harder as more people swarm into cities.

There is a need for a mobile product experience that appeals to millenials that makes it safe to find the ideal roommate in New York City. Identify pain points in the “find/keeping a good roommate journey” and propose ways to solve those pain points.

As college students, we understand the frustration that comes with finding off-campus housing and roommates, especially in New York. We wanted to design a platform that eases that process, while also ensuring users' safety when finding rentals.

Thus, Bunkie was born.

Research

Objectives

Bunkie is a mobile home service that aims to help current college students find suitable roommates and rooms off-campus in New York. It has two main product objectives:

  • Help college students find ideal roommates and rooms
  • Make roommate/room searching process safe and less time-consuming

Synthesis

Research Analysis

We gathered data from 33 respondents through short Google Form surveys that we distributed to college students in the NYC area.

90.9% of the respondents has no experience in using services to find roommates
97% of the respondents values the apartment location and price as the most important criteria
87% of the respondents values the roommate's living preferences as the most important criteria
57.6% of the respondents takes a month or more to find housing
