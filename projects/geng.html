Generation Girl

ROLE(S): Mentor, Course Builder

TECHNOLOGIES: HTML/CSS, Figma, repl.it, Python

DURATION: 2 Years (Jul 2019 to Jul 2021)

Generation Girl is a non-profit organization based in Jakarta, Indonesia under Yayasan Generasi Maju Berkarya (YGMB) that focuses on empowering young girls and building the next generation of Indonesian female leaders in STEM. From early 2019 through the Summer of 2021, I was involved at the organization as an instructor (mentor) and curriculum builder.

The classes I've taught are:

  • Build a Website I & II (HTML, CSS, JavaScript with React.js)
  • Computer Science 101 (Python)
  • UX Design
  • Applied Data Science/Intro to Machine Learning (image recognition app with Python, Flask, and TensorFlow

If you want a complete list of topics that I've covered during my classes, or want me to speak/teach at one of your events, please don't hesitate to reach out!
